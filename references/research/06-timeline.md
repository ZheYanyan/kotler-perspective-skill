# Timeline

## Early Life & Education

- **May 27, 1931** - Born in Chicago, Illinois, U.S. (Note: some sources cite May 23, but Wikipedia and primary biographical sources indicate May 27)
- **1948-1950** - Attended DePaul University for two years
- **1953** - Earned M.A. in Economics from the University of Chicago
- **1956** - Earned Ph.D. in Economics from MIT (Massachusetts Institute of Technology)
- **1960** - Completed postdoctoral work in Mathematics at Harvard University
- **1961** - Completed postdoctoral work in Behavioral Science at University of Chicago
- Studied under three Nobel Laureates in Economic Science: **Milton Friedman**, **Paul Samuelson**, and **Robert Solow**

## Career Milestones

- **Early 1960s** - Brief teaching career at Roosevelt University before joining Kellogg
- **1962** - Joined the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University
- **Later Career** - Named S.C. Johnson & Son Distinguished Professor of International Marketing at Kellogg
- **2018** - Retired from Northwestern University after 56-year tenure; continues as Professor Emeritus
- **Consulting** - Worked with major corporations including IBM, GE, AT&T, Honeywell, Merck, and Bank of America
- Regarded worldwide as **"The Father of Modern Marketing"**

## Key Publications

- **1967** - *Marketing Management* (first edition) - now in its 15th edition; world's most widely adopted graduate business textbook
- **Principles of Marketing** - companion textbook, also widely used globally
- **Marketing 4.0** - pioneering work on digital marketing transformation
- **Marketing 6.0: The Future is Immersive** (2024) - co-authored with Hooi Den Huan and Iwan Setiawan
- **2015** - *Confronting Capitalism*
- **2017** - *My Adventures in Marketing* (autobiography)
- **2018** - *Brand Activism: From Purpose to Action*
- **2023** - *Regeneration*
- **2024** - Multiple new publications including:
  - *Redefining Retailing* (co-authored with Giuseppe Stigliano)
  - *Transformative Marketing* (co-authored with V. Kumar)
  - *Humanism in Marketing* (co-authored with Waldermar Pfoertsch et al.)
  - *Reimagining Operational Excellence* (co-authored with Hermawan Kartajaya and Jacky Mussry)
- **Total output**: Has written 80+ books, with more than 5 million copies sold worldwide

## Thought Turning Points

- **1970s-1980s** - Developed the comprehensive marketing management framework that became the global standard
- **1990s-2000s** - Expanded beyond traditional marketing to address digital transformation and customer relationship management
- **2010s** - Began addressing the intersection of capitalism, social responsibility, and planetary sustainability
- **2021** - Co-founded the **Regenerative Marketing Institute**, shifting focus toward regenerative economics and sustainability

## Last 12 Months (2025-2026)

- **2024 publications** released in early 2025 or still in circulation - five major books including *Marketing 6.0* and *Redefining Retailing*
- Maintains active professional presence at age 94
- Resides in Longboat Key, Florida
- Continues to author new works and remain active in marketing thought leadership
- Professional website maintained at pkotler.org

## Current Status

- **Age**: 94 (as of 2025)
- **Title**: Professor Emeritus of International Marketing, Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University
- **Location**: Longboat Key, Florida
- **Activity**: Active author, thought leader, and consultant
- **Focus areas**: Regenerative marketing, capitalism critique, democratic systems, planetary sustainability, immersive marketing technology
